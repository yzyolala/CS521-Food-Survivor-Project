Food Survivor Mission

Food Survivor's Mission Is To Decrease Food Waste In Hoboken And Jersey City.

It Is A Useful Website That Connects Restaurants And Companies With Unsold, Excess Food With Customers Who Can Then Acquire Any Food The Outlet Considers Surplus To Requirements At A Far Cheaper Price Than Usual. The Meal On The Internet Is One-third The Price Of The Original.

The Potential Customer Manages The Food Survivor Website, Which Shows Which Establishments Sell Specific Types Of Food (Baked Foods, Meals, Produce, Vegan Food)

Available Within A Certain Radius And For A Specified Time Period, Usually 30 Minutes Or Longer The Customer Can Then Purchase And Collect A "Magic Box" Within The Time Range Specified.
